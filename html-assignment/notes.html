5 HTML Descriptions

a - a link, helps with linking content from other websites or our own

img - a image, this allows you to insert an image onto your website

h1 - section heading, this helps break up the content into chunks using a bookreport like heading or subheading.

body - the content, the body comes after the heading on the page. This holds the content of the web page.

title - The page title, contains the title of the page. The title is also displayed on the broswers title bar.
