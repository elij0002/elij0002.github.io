About Me

My name is Madalyn Elijah-Porter. I am 19 years old, and have recently moved to Ottawa. I moved here a couple weeks ago from Brantford, Ontario. I am the second eldest of my 3 siblings, I have an older sister and 2 younger brothers. My mom is a stay at home mom/wife and my dads an accountant. I also have a niece and nephew that I miss very much, as it goes both ways. I facetime everyone as often as I can.

My favorite food if I had to choose would have to be sushi. My favorite drink is either coffee or water.

I love being outdoors. I don't think I could choose one season as my favorite, as I love all 4 of them. Although the fall/Halloween time is my favorite time of year, I love dressing up and seeing everyone dressed up in their costumes. As well, the spooky decorations and scary movies are always fun.

In my free time I love to paint, watch movies or go for walks and listen to music. I don't think I have a specific music taste, I really enjoy all kinds of music. Just depends on my mood. This is a little bit about.
